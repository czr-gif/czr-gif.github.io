This is the first note in this blog space.

I plan to use it for ideas that are not quite papers, not quite projects, but still worth keeping: questions from reading, small observations from research, and reflections that become clearer when written down.

## Why keep a blog

Some thoughts need a lighter home than a publication. A blog makes room for drafts, uncertainty, and slow thinking.

Over time, this page can become a personal notebook: less polished than a CV, but more alive.
